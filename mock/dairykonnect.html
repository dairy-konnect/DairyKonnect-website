Frequently asked

Questions, answered.

Still curious? Reach out to our team — we typically respond within an hour during business days.

Does the farmer app work without internet?
Yes. Every entry — milk yield, vet booking, feed order — is saved offline first. The moment you reconnect, everything syncs in the background. We've built it for villages where 4G is a courtesy, not a guarantee.
How accurate is the AI quality check?
Our vision model achieves 96% agreement with traditional lactometer readings on SNF and fat. We re-train monthly on regional samples so accuracy improves as we expand.
Can I get my own branded app for my cooperative?
Absolutely. Our Enterprise plan includes white-label apps, your logo, brand colors and even your domain. Most cooperatives go live in 2-3 weeks.
How are farmers paid?
UPI auto-payouts based on your billing cycle (daily, weekly, or fortnightly). Payments are calculated against AI-graded quality and current market rates, then sent directly to the farmer's bank.
Is my data private?
Yes. We're SOC 2 aligned, host data in India, and never sell anonymized or identified data to third parties. You own your data. Full export available anytime.

Our story

A platform built on dirt roads, not in glass towers.

DairyKonnect started in a small village in Maharashtra, when our founder watched his uncle lose three weeks of milk earnings to a notebook that got lost in the rain. We've been building since.

Today we connect 12,000+ farmers, 340 vendors and a growing network of vets across 6 Indian states — and we're just warming up.

Milestones

From notebook to network.

2021

The first prototype

A weekend Excel sheet shared with 12 farmers in Pune district. They started recording milk, we started learning what they actually needed.

2022
